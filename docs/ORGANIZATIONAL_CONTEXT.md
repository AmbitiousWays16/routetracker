 ## Organization Mission & Vision
 
 **Mission:** WestCare empowers people to engage in healing, growth, and change.
 
 **Vision:** Uplifting the Human Spirit
 
 WestCare is a family of tax-exempt, nonprofit organizations that provide a wide spectrum of health and human services in both residential and outpatient settings.
